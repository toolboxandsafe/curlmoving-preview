About Curl Moving

Heavy-item specialists serving Arizona: vending machines, mechanic toolboxes, and gun safes—moved safely, on time, and fully insured.

Meet Ryan

I started Curl Moving because I saw a problem most people never think about—what do you do when you need to move something really heavy that a normal moving company isn't prepared for?

Now, every day I'm moving the kinds of items that scare off other movers. Toolboxes weighing close to a thousand pounds, soda machines that barely fit through a doorway, gun safes that can crack tile floors if they're not handled right—I've learned the methods and invested in the right equipment to make those moves safe and simple.

This isn't a big franchise; it's me and my brother, local to Arizona. I'm on every job, making sure your item is wrapped, strapped, protected, and delivered without damage.

Our Story

Curl Moving started with a simple idea: most movers aren't set up for the really heavy, awkward stuff. We invested in the right gear and methods so your safe, toolbox, or vending machine gets from A to B without drama—or damage.

Today we're the local team East Valley shops and homeowners call when the job needs pro equipment, careful planning, and insured handling.

Curl Moving has grown from word of mouth—neighbors, mechanics, shop owners, and families passing along my name after a safe, toolbox, or vending machine was placed exactly where they needed it. That's the part I love most: building trust one move at a time.

My goal is always the same: to treat your property like it's my own, show up when I say I will, and leave you feeling confident that the hardest part of your move was taken care of.
