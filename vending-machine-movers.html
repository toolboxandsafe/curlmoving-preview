Before Your Move

⚠️ Drink Machines

Must be unloaded completely. I won't move a drink machine with products inside - too heavy and risk of damage.

✓ Snack Machines

Don't have to unload, but note that chocolates may melt during transport on hot days.

Do I Need to Be Present?

✓ Double Doors

If your location has double doors with a removable middle piece, you don't have to be present.

⚠️ Single Door

If I have to move the vending machine through a single door, you need to be there.

I always notify 30 minutes before I arrive at the pickup location.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to unload the vending machines before moving?
For drink machines, yes - unload all of them, or I won't move the machine. Snack machines you don't have to, but note that chocolates may melt.
Do I have to be at pick up or drop off?
Sometimes. If I have to move the vending machine through a single door, you need to be there. Double doors that have a removable middle piece, you don't have to be present. I always notify 30 minutes before I arrive at the pickup location.
Does my vending machine go straight to drop off after pick up?
I may have other pick-ups that I need to do before your drop-off. I know the day before in the afternoon and will communicate the schedule with you.
How do I pay?
Usually after the service, but you can pay before. I accept Cash, Check, Venmo, Zelle, Cash App, Apple Pay, and Credit Card (add 3.5%).
