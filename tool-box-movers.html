Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to help with the move?
Nope! I just need to get access to both pick up and drop off and I can do the rest.
Should I empty my toolbox before the move?
You can leave it loaded! Just lock the drawers and remove anything loose on the outside. If you have a top or side hutch, let me know if it's bolted - I'll run a strap around it either way.
When do I pay?
You can pay after the service. I accept Cash, Check, Venmo, Zelle, Cash App.
Can you do same-day pickup and delivery?
Generally yes, but sometimes I have moves in-between especially for longer distance moves. I finalize the schedule the day before so you know exactly what will happen on move day.
What areas do you service?
I'm based in Gilbert and service all of Phoenix, Scottsdale, Mesa, Chandler, Tempe, and surrounding areas. I can go outside Phoenix metro too - just ask!
