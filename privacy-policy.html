Introduction

Curl Moving ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and physical addresses (for pickup and delivery)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only:

  • With service providers who assist in our operations (payment processors)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights
